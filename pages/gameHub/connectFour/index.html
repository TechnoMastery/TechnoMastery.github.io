Connect Four

Connect Four is a two-player connection game in which the players first choose a color and then take turns dropping colored discs into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid. The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four discs.

To play, click on the column where you want to drop your disc. The game will alternate turns between the two players.

Have fun!
