Naval Battle

Naval Battle is a thrilling multiplayer game where players command their own fleets of ships in epic naval battles. Strategize, outmaneuver your opponents, and sink their ships to claim victory!

Game Features

  • Multiplayer mode for up to 4 players on a grid
  • Local games : play all on the same computer !
  • Multiples game saving
  • Customizable ships and fleets
  • Dynamic weather and sea conditions
  • Try to kill your enemie by making alliances !

How to Play

  1. Create a game lobby directly on this page, or load another.
  2. Enter game name & player count
  3. Enter your names
  4. Select your fleet and customize your ships.
  5. Engage in naval battles with other players.
  6. Use strategy and tactics to outsmart your opponents.

Game Controls

  • Move your visor using the arrow keys.
  • Fire at the selected case with the spacebar.
  • Use [ZQSD] or [WASD] for first player and [UP LEFT DOWN RIGHT ARROWS] for second player to aim in battles
  • Use [SPACEBAR] to fire for first player and [0] for second player
