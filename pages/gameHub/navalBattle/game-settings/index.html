Players fleets

To change the fleet of the players, please enter the new fleet in the input below and enter the ID in the prompt. Note : you won't be able to change fleets after the game begin.


Avaible fleets list :
  • Default fleet : No power (default)
  • Ghost fleet : 2 times a game, you can turn invisible to not be touched by an attack. (ghost)
  • Undead fleet : Each time you sunk a ship, you can repair one of your boat by 1. You can't repair a sunken ship. If you don't have broken ships, you can't use that power. (undead)
