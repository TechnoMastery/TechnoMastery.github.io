Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. The possible shapes are rock (a fist), paper (an open hand), and scissors (a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V).

The game has three possible outcomes other than a tie: rock crushes scissors (rock wins), scissors cuts paper (scissors win), and paper covers rock (paper wins).
