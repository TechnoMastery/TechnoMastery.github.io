Mod changelog

Welcome to the changelog of the mod !

Here I put a retractable list of all features. This will be updated with new content. The single uptades will not be uptated.

2025

v2.0.0

Release date: 28-05-2052

Major update : full remake of the mod. Recoded from scratch directly in java, not using MCreator anymore. I put so a list of all features, but the referance that will change when new things come is this one.

Items :
  • Yeast : an ingredient item, used for the recipes of the holy bread and the devil bread.
  • Wind charged ingot : an ingrdient for the wind stick and the wind charged catalyzer.
  • Wind stick : a powerful stick created with wind charged ingot that can blow your enemies in the sky.
  • Half stick : a smaller stick used in some recipes.
  • Quarter stick : a very smaller stick used in some recipes.
  • Rotten leather : a simple way to craft leather.
  • Control panel : a very strong interface capable of getting to work many machines.
  • Conntrolled stick : a stick with a controlling power of anything else.
  • Conntrolled pickaxe : a pickaxe that can extract emeralds from a crafting grid.
  • Skullkery tool : a tool to manipulate skull and heads.
  • Sapphire : a basic / advanced item, depending of what you do with it.
  • Raw sapphire : raw version of the sapphire.
  • Creative set : the creative essence, creative residue, small creative nugget, creative nugget and creative ingot are used in recipes to make creative supplies. The creative block is also in this caregory.
  • Base of egg : this is a sub-product of creative ingot. It is able to create spawn eggs or spawners.
  • Spawner part : assembled with a base of egg, it make a spawner.
  • Dirt set : Extremely dry dirt, piece of extremely dry dirt, wet dirt and some blocks are part of the process of getting ressource dirt.
  • Ressource dirt : a dirt that can be used to get ressource from it.
  • Humid set : with some items & blocks from the dirt set, id make humid potions with flasks. Used in purifying dirt.
  • Dice : an item that you can throw, and it is a dice, so... You know it.
  • Hardened ingot : an ingot used for different craftings.
  • Slimy ingot : used to craft slimy things.
  • Slimy stick : used to craft slimy things.
  • Empty rune : a token that can be filled with something to make it useful.
  • Money rune : give you money on right click.
  • Xp rune : give you xp on right click.
  • Wood runes : give you wood of the selected type on right click (curently : oak, spruce).
  • Tree grower : it can turn an empty rune into a full rune of selected wood type.
  • Little copper nugget : can be turned into a full one of the create mod. Is in try to disapear.
  • Super charged ingot : an ingot to make the lightning sword.
  • Balls : in the "balls" creative mod tab, they turn into ressources for the majority of them.
  • Catalyzers : in the "balls" creative mod tab, they are used in the incubator to select the level of incubating.
  • Metal detector : it find (or not) the ores right below the right clicked block. Scan for all block in the Y axis, below the right clicked block.
  • Rubis : used in craft for rubinium sword
  • Rubinium : used in craft for rubinium sword
  • Fire diamond : used for craft of the fire sword
  • Fire stick : used for craft of the fire sword
  • Fire seeds & crop : useless, but you can harvest it if you want. You'll have lots when needed !
  • Pine cone : dropped from oak leaves, it can be used as fuel in furnaces.
  • Base of disc : used to create discs.
  • Sapphire staff : a purely decorative item.
  • Quantum core : made from a singularity, it have a powerful destructive power... But also in good. Use it carefully. Very carefully.
  • Stabilized quantum core : a more usefull version of the quantum core, for the price of some singularities...
  • Quantum staff : I firstly decorative staff. Will have a use in a future update, I just don't have any ideas for now. Go on the discord for suggestions !
  • Storm fragment : found in desert pyramid, it's used in the quantum core making.
